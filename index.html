"BEHIND THE SCREEN.
INTERIOR STYLIST"

The Moon Stores online store is a space for stylish accessories for beautiful homes. We are sure that the interior is created by carefully selected details, so in our collection you will find a huge range of decor items from around the world. Before a product enters our collection, we take it in our hands to understand and feel the beauty and power of this item in the interior.

The history of Moon Stores began several years ago, when the architect Olga Surkova and her husband Luis Enrique Masa, traveling the world, began to visit various exhibitions and collect interesting decor.
